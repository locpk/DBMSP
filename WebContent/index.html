Taking Bus

The transportation near the Bay-Flower Hotel is very convenient. There are three kinds of bus can reach the station called Bay-Flower Hotel station, and you will see our Hotel building.

Taking Subway

There is a subway station near the Bay-Flower Hotel , so the traffic is very convenient. If you want to the downtown, you just only spend 5 minutes to take subway. There three subway lines go through the Bay-Flower Hotel station

Driving Car

Bay-Flower Hotel is just a 45-minute drive from Zhuhai International Airport. Alternatively, visitors can take the Airport Express Line (AEL) to Bay-Flower Station where there is a free hotel shuttle bus to transport you to Bay-Flower Hotel for city breaks in a busy bustling international city. Direct airport coach transfers are also available.

The Tourist Attractions

There are some tourist attractions near our Hotel.

Zhuhai royal spa is a four-star spa leisure tourism resort, hot spring bathing soup, health rehabilitation, accommodation, conference, entertainment and other service projects, with dozens of hot spring pools and complete health facilities and health project form former imperial hot springs hot springs health system.

ChangLong Ocean Park is located in Zhuhai ocean kingdom, is the design of ChangLong group from the world top companies. ChangLong total construction project covers an area of ocean kingdom for more than the world's largest ocean park. The Ocean kingdom has eight themed areas, present different ocean view from all over the world and each area contains lots of amusement design, performance and ocean animal.
